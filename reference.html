Surfaces

Each surface has a role. Pick one before you place content.

Night

The default. Body, navigation, scene background.

--surface-night

Parchment

Reading, records, plans. Use sparingly — it's an object, not a wallpaper.

--surface-parchment

Stone

Structure, weight, boundary. Architectural elements.

--surface-stone

Ritual

Reserved for consequence. Only one per screen.

--surface-ritual
